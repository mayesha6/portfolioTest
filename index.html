about

The author of this site is a big career shifter. He started out as a fastfood crew then shifted in retail stores. When he got an opportunity to fly abroad, He then grab it and became forecourt attendant. At first, he was just curious about how to build a website. So, he started looking into it until this became his pastime during his freetime, abroad. Afterwards, he decided to enroll in a part time class, being in a graphic designing class, as he is also working there. As part of the closing season's class, he was honored a top rank, winning in a curriculum called "easter card making contest".

After three years abroad, he decided to take a short course class back in his home country, as he really clearly know now where is his passion. Fortunately enough, he passed a scholarship program and started learning Web developement.

Fast forward to present, He is now building this site for his collection of portfolios. As he now possess some skills he acquired from his previous job as an eCommerce python-Django developer for a year and a half. Where he started, zero knowledge with the said programming language.

By the way, the author of this site is also a core volunteer of python programming language community in the Philippines where you can visit here : pythonPH website
